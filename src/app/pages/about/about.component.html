About

Launched in 2023, Our technology platform connects customers, delivery partners, serving their multiple needs Customers use our platform to discover for delicious food , read and write customer generated reviews and view and upload photos, order food dining-out at restaurants. On the other hand, we provide restaurant partners with industry-specific marketing tools which enable them to engage and acquire customers to grow their business while also providing a reliable and efficient last service.
We also operate a one-stop procurement solution, Hyperpure, which supplies high quality ingredients and kitchen products to restaurant partners. We also provide our delivery partners with transparent and flexible earning opportunities.
